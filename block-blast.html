Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

OMT Games ("we", "our", or "us") built the Block Blast Puzzle app as a free application. This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information Collection and Use

Block Blast Puzzle is designed with privacy in mind. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to external servers.

  • Game Data: Your scores, high scores, and game settings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.
  • Preferences: App settings (dark mode, sound, vibration) are stored locally on your device.

Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous usage data and crash reports. This helps us improve app stability and user experience. This data does not identify individual users and includes:

  • App usage statistics (screens viewed, features used)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Crash logs and performance data

You can learn more about Google's data practices at Google Privacy Policy.

Advertising

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads:

  • Advertising ID: A unique, user-resettable identifier for advertising purposes.
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, screen size, and network type.
  • Usage data: Ad interaction data (views, clicks) and general app usage patterns.
  • IP address: Used for approximate location-based ad targeting and fraud prevention.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

For more information, see the Google Ads Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)
  • Google AdMob (Advertising)

Permissions

  • Internet: Required for displaying advertisements and sending anonymous analytics data.
  • AD_ID: Used by Google AdMob to serve relevant advertisements.

Data Security

All user game data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers that store your personal data. Network communication is limited to third-party services (AdMob, Firebase) and is encrypted in transit.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
